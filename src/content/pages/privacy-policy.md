**Last updated: June 2026**
 
This privacy policy explains how The Greyhound Hub ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects information when you visit thegreyhoundhub.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).
 
If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at [hello@thegreyhoundhub.com](mailto:hello@thegreyhoundhub.com).
 
---
 
## Who We Are
 
The Greyhound Hub is a free, non-commercial resource for people interested in adopting or caring for greyhounds. The site is operated by The Greyhound Hub team and is not affiliated with any commercial organisation.
 
---
 
## What Data We Collect and Why
 
### 1. Website Analytics (Google Analytics 4)
 
We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. This includes information such as which pages you visit, how long you spend on the site, what device and browser you use, and your approximate location (country or region level only).
 
This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. It is processed by Google and may be stored on servers outside the UK.
 
**Lawful basis:** Legitimate interests — understanding how our site is used helps us improve it for visitors.
 
You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the [Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout). For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
 
---
 
### 2. Behaviour Analytics (Microsoft Clarity)
 
We use Microsoft Clarity to better understand how visitors interact with our site. Clarity records information such as mouse movements, clicks, scrolling behaviour, and session replays. This helps us identify areas of the site that may be confusing or difficult to use.
 
Please be aware that Clarity captures actual on-screen interactions. We recommend you do not enter any sensitive personal information into any forms on this site before accepting analytics cookies.
 
**Lawful basis:** Legitimate interests — understanding user behaviour helps us improve the experience for visitors.
 
For more information, see [Microsoft's Privacy Policy](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-gb/privacystatement).
 
---
 
### 3. Contact Forms
 
If you contact us via our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry and will not share it with any third parties.
 
We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months, after which they are deleted.
 
**Lawful basis:** Legitimate interests — responding to enquiries from visitors.
 
---
 
### 4. Newsletter Signup
 
If you sign up for our newsletter, we collect your email address and, if provided, your name. We use this information to send you updates about new content on The Greyhound Hub.
 
We will never share your email address with third parties or use it for any purpose other than sending you content you have signed up for.
 
You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send you.
 
**Lawful basis:** Consent — you actively opt in when signing up.

## Cookies
 
Our site uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — for analytics and functionality purposes.
 
| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Google Analytics (_ga, _gid) | Anonymous site usage analytics | Up to 2 years |
| Microsoft Clarity | Session recording and heatmaps | Up to 1 year |
 
When you first visit our site, you will be asked to accept or decline non-essential cookies via our cookie consent banner. You can change your preferences at any time.
 
You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how the site functions.

## Your Rights Under UK GDPR
 
Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:
 
- **Right of access** — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Right to rectification** — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure** — you can ask us to delete your personal data
- **Right to restrict processing** — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
- **Right to object** — you can object to our processing of your data
- **Right to data portability** — you can request your data in a portable format
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [hello@thegreyhoundhub.com](mailto:hello@thegreyhoundhub.com). We will respond within 30 days.
