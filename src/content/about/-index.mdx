We created the Greyhound Hub with the aim of building a robust resource hub that is useful for all greyhound owners, both new and old.

## Why we started the Greyhound Hub

After adopting our own ex-racer, Winston, we felt overwhelmed by all of the information which is out there - and at the same time, struggled to find the information we needed when we needed it. There are some amazing greyhound charities and communities out there, but the advice can be fragmented or buried in obscure forum threads from 10+ years ago.

## What we do

We create helpful resources based on our own experience as greyhound owners. We also scour abandoned blogs, community Facebook groups, Reddit threads, and more...so you don't have to. We speak to members of the greyhound community to lean on their experience and expertise, so that we can share that with the world. Wherever we source information form elsewhere, **this is credited to the original owner or contributor.** You can read more about our editorial policy [here](/# "editorial policy").

## Who we are

We don't represent a rescue or rehoming centre. We're not a business or shop. We don't represent any racing kennels or breeders. We're just two people who love greyhounds and want to share all of the knowledge we've accumulated!
